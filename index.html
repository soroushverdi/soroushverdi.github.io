I am a PhD Candidate in Marketing at the University of Groningen.

My primary research interest lies in Judgment and Decision Making (JDM). As artificial intelligence becomes an inseparable part of our daily lives, I am increasingly interested in the psychology of AI,  how people perceive and interact with it, and how these perceptions influence their behaviors and decisions . This ties closely to my broader interest in judgment and decision-making research.

I am also interested in nudging and framing, that how subtle differences in how information is presented can systematically influence the choices people make .
