I am Soroush Verdi, PhD Candidate in Marketing at the University of Groningen. My research focuses on consumer behavior and judgment and decision-making, with a particular interest in cognitive biases and how behavioral interventions, such as nudging and framing, shape human perception and choice. During my PhD, I am investigating how people’s perceptions of Artificial Intelligence (AI) influence their decisions and actions.

I hold a Research Master’s degree in Economics and Business from the University of Groningen. I also contribute to teaching at both the Bachelor's and Master's levels within the Faculty of Economics and Business.
